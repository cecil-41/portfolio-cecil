About Me

I have recently completed my Honors degree in BComHons Information Systems from the University of Johannesburg, achieving it with distinction. Prior to this, I graduated with a BCom in Information Systems, also with distinction.

I am a Developer with experience in a variety of programming languages, including HTML5, CSS, PHP, C#, JavaScript, MySQL, Microsoft SQL Server, and others. With over three years of hands-on experience, I have not only mastered these languages but have also shared my knowledge by teaching and actively engaging in projects.

Coding isn't just a job for me; it's a passion. I'm always learning and keeping up with the latest in tech because I believe that's the secret sauce to successful software development.
